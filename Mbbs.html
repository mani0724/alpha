MBBS - Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery

The curriculum in MBBS is designed in such a way that MBBS degree holders can choose a specialisation for further majoring and practising medicine. The program includes subjects such as anatomy, pharmacology, pathology as well as community health & medicine, paediatrics, and surgery.

Duration- total 5.5 years which includes 1-year mandatory internship.

Eligibility - Students should have cleared 10+2 exam with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology and English as core subject in both Classes 11 and 12 from a recognised boardwith the minimum aggregate of UR - 50%, OBC/SC/ST - 40%, PWD - 45% and should be 17+ years as on 31st December of the year of admission. Student should qualify National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) which is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA).
